Breakfast Ideas

Mornings can be hectic, but a good breakfast sets the tone for the day.
Try these quick ideas:

  • Overnight Oats: Mix oats, milk, honey, and fruit in a jar overnight.
  • Avocado Toast: Spread mashed avocado on whole-grain toast and top with eggs.
  • Smoothie Bowl: Blend bananas, berries, and yogurt, then top with granola.
  • Egg Muffins: Beat eggs, add veggies and cheese, then bake in muffin tins.
  • Peanut Butter Banana Toast: A classic energy booster!
